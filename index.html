Roh Zi

For someone that’s been exposed to the _ of west district; Roh Zi still manages to wear a smile brighter than the neon signs littering the street outside her bar. First met the kid working the case that cost me my badge. Her old man called her in M.I.A. a few times and I noticed the higher ups didn’t seem too interested in tracking her down. Only took a day, and a few squabbles with some local info bros for me to track down the traffickers that snatched her for their meat puppet operation. Managed to get her out, but had to bag and tag a few bullet-stoppers. In the process one of the clients managed to get greased. Turns out he was a somebody, and my career got swept under the rug with the rest of the dirt during the cover up. Hey at least Roh Zi cuts me a break renting office space above The Jive Dive
